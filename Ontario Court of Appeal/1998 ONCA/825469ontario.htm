                      ENDORSEMENT
                                

[1]  The appellants did not apply for a mistrial or to reopen the
case.  We are not satisfied that the appellants have established
that this document should be admitted as fresh evidence. There is
no material before us to show that this invoice could not have
been produced through the exercise of due diligence. On its face
it would appear that a copy of the document was in the
appellants' possession. The appellants have produced no evidence
to show that was not the case.


[2]  Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed with costs.
