                      E N D O R S E M E N T
                                

[1]  The broadcast complained of reported that there were
allegations that the appellant had misstated his credentials. The
appellant has acknowledged in cross-examination that there were
indeed such allegations. Given the admission that the report
complained of is true, there can be no genuine issue for trial.


[2]  While this issue was not raised in the notice of motion
below, it properly arose in the cross-examination of the
appellant on his affidavit filed below. There was full
opportunity for both parties to deal with this issue before the
motions judge.


[3]  The appeal must be dismissed with costs.
