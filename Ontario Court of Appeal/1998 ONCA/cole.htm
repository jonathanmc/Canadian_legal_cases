                          ENDORSEMENT


[1]  In our view, given the trial judge's finding of credibility
against the husband, it was open to the trial judge to value the
husband's business interest as he did.  Secondly, it was within
his discretion, given the evidence before him, to make the
discount for tax from the lump sum payment as he did.  We see no
palpable error in either finding and the appeal must be dismissed
with costs.
