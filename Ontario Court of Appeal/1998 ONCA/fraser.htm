                          ENDORSEMENT

[1]  The appellant has not shown any error in principle on the
part of the assessment officer as revealed in his report. We
cannot assume that the absence of a breakdown in those reasons as
to the individual amounts deducted totaling $78,000 demonstrates
that the deductions in the solicitor's bill of costs were not
done in accordance with the appropriate principles mentioned in
his reasons. Accordingly, there is no error on the part of
Festeryga J. in declining to interfere with that assessment.


[2]  The appeal is dismissed with costs, fixed at $1,500.
