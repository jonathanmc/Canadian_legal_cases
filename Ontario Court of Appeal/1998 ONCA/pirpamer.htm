                      ENDORSEMENT

[1]  In our view, the obligation to pay the taxes was not a pre-
condition to exercising the option to reconvey. That option was
not null and void. In this respect we disagree with both
Boland J. However, we agree with her that on the material before
her the applicant had not demonstrated compliance with the
Planning Act so as to entitle him to an order to reconvey.


[2]  We therefore allow the appeal to the extent of replacing the
first paragraph of the judgment below with the following:
          
          This court orders and adjudges that the
          option to reconvey registered on August 20,
          1975 as Instrument No. 76190 in the Land
          Registry Office for the Registry Division of
          Toronto, more particularly described in
          Schedule "A" attached hereto between Frank
          Pirpamer as Grantor and Pasquale Zanette and
          Lydia Zanette as Grantees is unenforceable by
          reason of the conditions imposed which the
          optionor cannot satisfy.

[3]  We would order no costs of the appeal.
