                          ENDORSEMENT


[1]  The appellant seeks a stay of the order of Beaulieu J. dated
October 16, 1998 pending disposition of her appeal. Pursuant to
the terms of the Hague Convention, Beaulieu J. ordered the
immediate return of the parties' child to California subject to
an interim custody order in favour of the appellant for a period
of 30 days if she returned with the child. At issue on the appeal
is whether Beaulieu J. erred in finding that the appellant had
not successfully demonstrated that the exception under Article
13(b) applied (return of child not mandated where there is a
grave risk that the return "would expose the child to physical or
psychological harm or otherwise place the child in an intolerable
situation").


[2]  In my view, the application should be granted. The appeal
raises a serious issue as to what extent the evidence of harm
presented on the application went to the merits of the custody
hearing or to establishing the grounds for an exception under
Article 13 (b). If the stay is not granted, the appeal will be
nugatory and in this sense, the appellant will suffer irreparable
harm. On the other hand, the appeal can be heard expeditiously
and any further delay can be kept at a minimum.  Hence the
balance of convenience favours the appellant.


[3]  The respondent argues that a more stringent test should be
met before a stay is granted given Canada's obligation under the
treaty to deal with such matters expeditiously. The nature of the
proceeding is obviously a relevant consideration on the stay
application. It is my view, however, that the treaty obligation
to secure "the prompt return of children" in appropriate cases
does not translate into a more stringent test on a stay
application. Rather, the obligation should be fulfilled by
hearing the appeal as expeditiously as possible.


[4]  Therefore, the application is granted. The order of Beaulieu
J. is stayed pending the hearing of the appeal. The appeal is
ordered expedited. Both parties are forthwith directed to seek a
date for hearing on an expedited basis from the Registrar of this
Court or his delegate.
