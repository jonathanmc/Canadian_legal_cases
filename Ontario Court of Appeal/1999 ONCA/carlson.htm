                      ENDORSEMENT
                                


[1]   We  are not persuaded that the defendant has satisfied  his
onus  of showing that there is no genuine issue for trial.  While
the  evidence  may  be thin, there remains  an  issue  about  the
respondent  being  distracted and not keeping a proper  look-out,
and  whether  his  failure to do so may have contributed  to  the
appellant’s injuries.
[2]  The appeal is allowed, the order of Justice Lissaman is set
aside, and the motion for summary judgment is dismissed with
costs here and on the motion.
