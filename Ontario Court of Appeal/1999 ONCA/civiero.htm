                      ENDORSEMENT
[1]  The appellants have not persuaded us that the material
before us demonstrates the creation of the trust on which they
rely with the sufficient certainty of intention, subject-matter,
and object.  The registered ownership of the land continues to
unequivocally show Mr. Civiero as the beneficial owner of the
property to the extent of twenty-five per cent.
[2]  In light of this conclusion it is unnecessary for us to deal
with the issue of whether the respondent can rely on the Statute
of Frauds.
[3]  The appeal must be dismissed with costs.
