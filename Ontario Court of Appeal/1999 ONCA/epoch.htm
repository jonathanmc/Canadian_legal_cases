                      ENDORSEMENT

                                                               
[1]  Although the trial judge found that the respondent was
involved in three incidents of misconduct, she also found that in
all of the circumstances the respondent had not acted
dishonestly. She had the advantage of seeing and hearing the
witnesses and we are not in a position to conclude that she was
wrong in her finding in this respect.


[2]  We do not consider that the trial judge=s interventions in
questioning two of the appellant's witnesses indicated bias on
her part.


[3]  The appeal is dismissed with costs. The cross-appeal is
withdrawn without costs.
