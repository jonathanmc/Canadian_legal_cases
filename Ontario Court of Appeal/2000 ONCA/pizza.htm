ENDORSEMENT

[1]  We agree entirely with the reasons and conclusions of Sharpe
J. writing for a unanimous Divisional Court.  In our view, our
intervention is not warranted.  Accordingly, the appeal is
dismissed with costs fixed at $6,000 for the Toronto Star
respondents and $4,000 for the respondent Rafati.
