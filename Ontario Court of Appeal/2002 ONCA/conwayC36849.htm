A D D E N D U M

BY THE COURT:

[1]               Costs submission have been made by the appellant, the respondent choosing not to respond.  The appellant is entitled to partial-indemnity costs to the date of the settlement offer and to substantial-indemnity costs thereafter to the conclusion of trial.  The Rule 49 offer made before trial does not apply to proceedings in the Court of Appeal.  We are not in a position to assess costs for the trial proceedings on the material presented and they will be referred for assessment.  We fix the partial-indemnity costs in the Court of Appeal at $15,000 plus disbursements, plus G.S.T.
