ENDORSEMENT   O N   COSTS

[1]               The respondent, the successful party both before Backhouse J. and in this court, has asked us to fix the costs of the motion and of the appeal.

[2]               In our view, the costs of the motion is a matter for the motions judge. 

[3]               We have considered the written submissions of both sides on the costs in this court.  In our view, costs should be awarded to the respondent on a partial-indemnity basis.  Although we accept that Mr. Ayers and Mr. Smith devoted the time for this appeal each has recorded, we think a fair figure for the respondent’s costs is $7,500 plus assessable disbursements and G.S.T.
