ENDORSEMENT   O N   COSTS

[1]               We have had the benefit of written submissions on costs.  We substantially agree with the submissions of Mr. Linley on behalf of the interveners.  We therefore order that the Township is entitled to total costs of $10,000 for the two appeals.  Of this amount, $7,000 shall be paid by Donald Clark and $3,000 shall be paid by the interveners.
