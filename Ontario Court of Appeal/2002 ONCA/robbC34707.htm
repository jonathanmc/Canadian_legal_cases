ENDORSEMENT ON COSTS

[1]               Our reasons for judgment in this appeal were released on November 29, 2001.

[2]               In paragraph 213 of our reasons, we noted that counsel had made no submissions respecting the costs of the trial in the event that the appeals by the CRCS and Canada were allowed, and invited the parties to make written submissions regarding such costs.

[3]               We have now received and considered the written submissions of the parties, and make the following disposition of the costs at trial:

(a)         the Canadian Red Cross Society shall have its costs on a party-and-party scale from the plaintiffs;

(b)        there will be no costs to or against Canada; and

(c)        there will be no costs to or against Ontario.

[4]               We would not disturb the disposition of Bayer’s costs ordered by the trial judge.

Signed: “M.A. Catzman J.A.”

              “Louise Charron J.A.”


[1] Osborne A.C.J.O., who retired from the court on September 14, 2001, took no part in the subject of this endorsement.
