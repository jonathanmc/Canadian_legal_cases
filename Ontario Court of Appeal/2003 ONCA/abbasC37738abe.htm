APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] We did not call on the respondent except in relation to the trial judge's holding with respect to the "deli" business.

[2] The holding is in para. 92 of the reasons. We are satisfied that the respondent's evidence and the appellant's own conduct in relation to the deli business provided an evidentiary basis for the trial judge's finding. We cannot interfere.

[3] The appeal is dismissed. Costs to the respondent fixed in the amount of $2,000.
