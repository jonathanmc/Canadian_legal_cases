ENDORSEMENT

[1] In his able argument for the moving party, Mr. Wise said everything that could be said in support of the motion. However, we are satisfied that MacPherson J.A. was correct in dismissing the motion to extend the time for appealing. The relief now sought by the moving party could and should have been raised on the appeals to this court on October 31, 2000. That relief is, therefore, subsumed in this court's dismissal of those appeals.

[2] Accordingly, this motion is dismissed.
