APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] The decision of this court in R. v. Stephens (1995), 102 C.C.C. (3d) 416 is dispositive of this case, from which it is indistinguishable in principle. The First Nations constables in the case at bar had jurisdiction arising from the Police Services Act and their appointment by the Commissioner of the O.P.P. Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.
