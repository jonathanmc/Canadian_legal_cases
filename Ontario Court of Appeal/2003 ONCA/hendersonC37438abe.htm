APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] In our view, there was ample evidence to support the trial judge's ruling that the appellant was voluntarily unemployed as of September 1993. The appellant did not object to the remainder of the charge to the jury. There is no basis to interfere with the jury's verdict. The appeal is dismissed.

[2] Costs to the respondent are fixed in the amount of $4,000 inclusive of disbursements and G.S.T., if requested.
