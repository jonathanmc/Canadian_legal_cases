APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] In our view, the sentence imposed of one year was inadequate given the respondent's lengthy criminal record. However, this court has generally been reluctant to re-incarcerate an offender who has served the custodial portion of his sentence, being some eight months in this case.

[2] In our view, the circumstances here do not warrant the respondent's re-incarceration.

[3] Leave to appeal allowed but the appeal is dismissed.
