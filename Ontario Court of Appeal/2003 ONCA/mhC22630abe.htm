On appeal from the conviction by Justice D. R. Main of the Ontario Court of Justice dated May 1, 1995 and from the sentence imposed by Justice D. R. Main dated August 1, 1995.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] There was evidence to support the verdict and we therefore reject the argument directed to unreasonable verdict. The trial judge expressed himself inappropriately concerning reasonable doubt but, in the end, we are satisfied that he was merely responding to the form of argument put by counsel for the accused and that he was in fact satisfied beyond a reasonable doubt.

[2] The appeal is dismissed.
