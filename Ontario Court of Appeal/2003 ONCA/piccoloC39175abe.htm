APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] Cases of this nature invariably turn on their own facts. On the facts as found by the trial judge in this case, for which there was support in the evidence, there is no basis for interfering with his conclusion that a fiduciary relationship existed between the appellant and the respondent.

[2] The appeal is therefore dismissed.

[3] Costs to be agreed by counsel or in the failure to agree, written submissions will be made to this court.
