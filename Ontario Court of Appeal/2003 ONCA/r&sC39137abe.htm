APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] We agree with Hoy J. that the Agreement and the Bills of Lading had to be read together and therefore the appellant had to give 60 days written notice of liability for a claim based on delay.

[2] The appeal is dismissed, without costs. Costs of the motion before the Deputy Registrar fixed at $535 inclusive of disbursements and GST payable by the appellant to the respondent.
