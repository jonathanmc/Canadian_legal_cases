APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] The Crown concedes that the appeal as to conviction should be allowed with respect to the charge of conspiracy to traffic and that a verdict of acquittal be substituted. We agree.

[2] The appeal as to the two convictions of trafficking is dismissed as we see no merit in this appeal as the identification evidence was overwhelming.

[3] The Crown also concedes that the sentence must be reduced given the acquittal on the conspiracy charge and agrees that the sentence be reduced to time served which was the equivalent of 14 months.

[4] In the circumstances we agree with the Crown's submissions. Leave to appeal sentence granted, the appeal is allowed and the sentence is reduced to time served.
