On appeal from the order of Justice Matlow dated July 3, 2002.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] The appellant was not involved in the accident and she did not have first party insurance. She was not entitled to statutory benefits under any insurance policy. She therefore had no recourse against the Fund under s. 6 of the Motor Vehicle Accident Claims Act.

[2] Accordingly, for the reasons given by the motions judge, with which we agree, the appeal is dismissed with costs fixed in the amount of $1,500.00.
