APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               Despite her effective submissions, we are not persuaded that there is any basis for interfering with Cameron J.’s decision.  There was ample evidence on which he could base his finding of liability, and it is not open to us to retry that finding.

[2]               While we considered the fresh evidence, we cannot say that it would have altered his findings. 

[3]               It was open to the trail judge, based on the submissions, to strike the jury notice. 

[4]               The appeal is dismissed but, in the circumstances, without costs.
