APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] The appellant concedes that this court's decision in R. v. Hatfield (1997), 115 C.C.C. (3d) 47 is dispositive of this appeal and that, unless this court is prepared to overrule the ratio in Hatfield, the appeal must be dismissed.

[2] Counsel for the appellant has not persuaded us that there is any basis upon which this court could, or should, depart from Hatfield.

[3] The appeal is dismissed.
