APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] We see no basis for interfering with the findings of fact made by the trial judge in this. They were available on the record and the trial judge made no palpable and overriding error in his appreciation of the evidence.

[2] The appeal is therefore dismissed.

[3] The respondent is entitled to his costs fixed in the amount of $10,000.00.
