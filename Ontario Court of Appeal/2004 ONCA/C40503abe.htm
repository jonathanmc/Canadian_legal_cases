On appeal from the order of Justice Helen M. Pierce of the Superior Court of Justice dated July 10, 2003.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] There are two issues on this appal:

(1) whether the appellant was acting qua councillor in the context of the suit brought against him by various electors in relation to his election expenses; and

(2) whether the resolution of the City Council dated February 10, 2003 relating to the payment of the appellant's legal fees was ultra vires.

[2] On both issues, we agree entirely with the analysis and conclusions of Pierce J. Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed with costs fixed at $5000, inclusive of disbursements and GST.
