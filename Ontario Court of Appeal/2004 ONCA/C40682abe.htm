APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] This is an appeal from the order of Byers J. dated October 23, 2003. The matters that the appellant asks this court to address - namely, pension valuation, disclosure of the respondent's employee benefits and remitting the matter for a further settlement conference - relate, in the main, to the order of Dunbar J. dated February 15, 2002 from which no appeal was taken.

[2] This appeal cannot be used as a collateral attack on the order of Dunbar J. There being nothing raised in the appeal which is properly before us, the appeal is dismissed. No order as to costs.
