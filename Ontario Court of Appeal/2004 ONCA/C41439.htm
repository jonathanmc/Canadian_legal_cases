On appeal from the judgment of Justice Jean A. Forget of the Superior Court of Justice, sitting without a jury, dated January 28, 2004.

ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We are satisfied that the motion judge did not err in his conclusion that the Royal Trust Corporation of Canada is entitled to a discharge upon payment to Rockcliffe Park Realty Limited of the sum of $25,000.00 plus interest.  Section 93(2) of the Land Titles Act, R.S.O. 1990, c. L.5 provides:

93. (2)  A charge that secures the payment of money shall state the amount of the principal sum that it secures.

[2]               In our view, the mandatory language of s. 93(2) dictates the result.

[3]               The appeal is therefore dismissed with costs fixed in the amount of $5,000.00, inclusive of disbursements and G.S.T.
