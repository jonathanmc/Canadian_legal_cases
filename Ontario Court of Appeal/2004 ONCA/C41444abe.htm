On appeal from the sentence imposed by Justice A. Ross Webster of the Ontario Court of Justice dated February 17, 2004.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] There was a miscommunication in this case between the two counsel and the trial judge. Counsel intended to make a joint submission of a sentence of six months less time served.

[2] The trial judge explicitly stated that he was "prepared to accept the joint submission based on the fact that you have spent 172 days in pre-trial custody". However, he then imposed an additional sentence of six months. .

[3] The Crown concedes that in light of these circumstances the appeal should be allowed, the sentence should be set aside, and a sentence of time served should be imposed.
