APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1] The trial judge's conclusion that the appellant had not established that the money he received from Zigman was a gift is well-supported by the record, as is his conclusion that the storage of property at the abandoned school did not constitute a bailment.

[2] Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed with costs of $7000, inclusive of disbursements and GST.
