APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               All the  eleven relevant claims have been quantified under the C.C.A.A. because of the delay under the CLC process.

[2]               The voters approved the Plan at 99.8%.

[3]               Subsequently, the sanction order was issued and no appeal was taken from that order. The order dismissed all other claims.

[4]               We do not accept the argument of the Attorney General that the issue here is one of jurisdiction. It is one of legal correctness.

[5]               In our view, this appeal is clearly moot. This is no controversy left between the relevant claimants and Attorney General.

[6]               As to the exercise of our discretion, we are left with a totally academic issue that could only affect a very limited number of employees, namely non-union employees of federally regulated companies.

[7]               Moreover, legislation has been tabled that addresses, at least in part, this issue.

[8]               In these circumstances, we would not exercise our discretion. The appeal is dismissed as being moot with costs fixed at $10,000.00
