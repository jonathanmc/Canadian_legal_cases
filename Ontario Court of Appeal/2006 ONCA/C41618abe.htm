On appeal from conviction by Justice Douglas McDermid of the Superior Court of Justice, sitting without a jury, dated February 25, 2004 and sentence imposed March 4, 2004.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appeal is essentially fact-driven. The trial judge gave full and cogent reasons in support of his conclusion that the complainant was incapable of consenting to sexual activity due to her advanced state of intoxication. In coming to that conclusion, the trial judge considered the evidence as a whole. In our view, his conclusion is amply supported by the evidence and we see no basis for interfering with it. Accordingly, the appeal for conviction is dismissed. The appeal from sentence is dismissed as abandoned.
