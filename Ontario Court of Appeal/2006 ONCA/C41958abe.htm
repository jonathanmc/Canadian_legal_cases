APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The sentencing judge had read the pre‑sentence report and heard the submissions of counsel.  In our opinion the sentencing judge’s statement that there were no mitigating factors was his way of saying that he had considered the mitigating factors and rejected them.  The young age of the victim, the fact that there was penetration, and the threat of violence lead us to conclude that the sentence is fit even having regard to the totality principle.

[2]               Accordingly, while leave to appeal sentence is granted the appeals as to sentence are dismissed.  The conviction portion of the appeal was abandoned.
