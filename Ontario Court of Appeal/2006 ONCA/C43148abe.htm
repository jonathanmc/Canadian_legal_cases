APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The trial judge was alive to the frailties of the identification evidence.  In our opinion the combination of identification evidence and the confirmatory evidence render the verdict one that a properly instructed trier of fact acting judicially could reasonably have rendered and the summary conviction appeal judge did not err in dismissing the appeal.  The appeal is dismissed.
