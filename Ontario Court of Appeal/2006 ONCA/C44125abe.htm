On appeal from the sentence imposed by Justice S. Chapnik of the Superior Court of Justice dated September 12, 2005.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               Mr. Tice has said all there is that could be said on behalf of the appellant.  We cannot agree that the trial judge misused the information provided in the pre-sentence report.  The absence of remorse was a relevant consideration in considering whether  a conditional sentence was appropriate.  We see no error in principle in the sentencing.  In any event, we think the sentence imposed was a fit sentence. 

[2]               The appeal is dismissed.
