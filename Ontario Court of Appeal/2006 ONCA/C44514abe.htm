APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We are not persuaded that the sentencing judge made any error in principle or misapprehended the evidence.  The inference that the appellant was a trusted member of the organization was open to her on the record.

[2]               Nor are we persuaded that a four-year sentence is outside an acceptable range.  Given the appellant’s role and given that this was a very large scale marijuana operation, the sentence was fit.

[3]               Leave to appeal sentence is granted but the appeal is dismissed.
