On appeal from the orders of Justice James M. Spence of the Superior Court of Justice, dated January 11, 2006 and March 22, 2006.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               Gold Financial Corp. appeals two orders of Spence J. and asks that we order one-half of the proceeds of the sale of the Puslinch land be paid into court.  We do not agree with Gold’s position.

[2]               Spence J. concluded that neither the Notice nor the Share Pledge Agreement is entitled to any weight and that Gold’s GSA did not support any claim to an interest in the land.

[3]               On the record before us, he was fully entitled to come to these conclusions and we decline to interfere with them.

[4]               Both of the orders under appeal are consistent with these conclusions. 

[5]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed, with costs on a partial indemnity basis.
