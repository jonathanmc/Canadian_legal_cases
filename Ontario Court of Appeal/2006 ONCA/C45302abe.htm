APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The respondent imposed a penalty on the appellant in disciplinary proceedings against him. Rather than appealing that decision, the appellant brought this lawsuit claiming that the discipline decision was wrong and that the respondent defamed him by publishing its decision on the Internet. The appellant asserts that the respondent acted in bad faith in his claim in this action. To the extent that the motion judge held otherwise, we respectfully disagree.

[2]               However, having failed to appeal the respondent’s decision, it was not open to the appellant to bring this action as it constitutes an abuse of process and a collateral attack on the decision.

[3]               Accordingly the appeal is dismissed. Costs to the respondent fixed at $1,000 inclusive of G.S.T. and disbursements.
