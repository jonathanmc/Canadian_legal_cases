On appeal from the order of Justice Margaret P. Eberhard of the Superior Court of Justice dated June 30, 2006.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We agree with Eberhard J. that the appellant is attempting to relitigate issues decided by Logan J. We are also satisfied that the appellant was properly declared to be a vexatious litigant. Eberhard J. set out a timetable and procedure to allow for bringing an application to vary spousal support. We see no reason to interfere with this order.

[2]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed with costs payable to the respondent fixed in the amount of $4,000 inclusive of G.S.T. and disbursements.
