On appeal from the decision of the Divisional Court in an application for judicial review.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We agree with the Attorney General’s submission that this appeal must be quashed.  The judgment under appeal is a decision of the Divisional Court and s. 6(1) of the Courts of Justice Act is determinative.  The Divisional Court properly decided the entire matter as the Divisional Court.  The challenge to the constitutional validity of the underlying regulation and statute was properly before the Divisional Court as an aspect of the application for judicial review and it is too late for the appellant to reconstitute the matter as a Rule 14 application before a judge of the Superior Court.  Costs to the Attorney General fixed at $750 and to the Dairy Farmers of Ontario fixed at $250, inclusive of GST and disbursement.
