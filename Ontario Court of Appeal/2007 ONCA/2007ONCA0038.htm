APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               Notwithstanding the facts – relied on by the appellant – that the sentencing judge failed to refer to the appellant’s guilty plea or to the jump principle as factors mitigating the sentence, the sentence imposed on the appellant was within the range and an appropriate response to the number and nature of the offences for which he was convicted.

[2]               The appeal is dismissed.
