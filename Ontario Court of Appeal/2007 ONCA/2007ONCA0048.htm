On appeal from the judgment of Justice J. A. Desotti of the Superior Court of Justice dated April 7, 2005.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appellant appeals from the judgment of Desotti J. with respect to the matrimonial home, spousal support (continuing at $2500 per month) and child support for one son, David.  We see no basis for interfering with any of the trial judge’s dispositions which strike us as fair and reasonable on the basis of the record before him.  The appeal is dismissed.

[2]               The parties are agreed that the equalization subsequent to the sale of the matrimonial home is accurately set out in the Respondent’s Book: Net Sale Proceeds and Equalization Calculations, namely husband – $112,305.95, wife – $145,650.21.

[3]               The respondent is entitled to his costs of the appeal, including costs reserved to this court by Desotti J., fixed at $20,000 inclusive of disbursements and GST.
