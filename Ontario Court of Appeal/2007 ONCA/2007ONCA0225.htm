COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The decision of this court was released on January 29, 2007. 

[2]               We have received the cost submission of counsel and considered them.  At first instance the parties did not suggest that as this was a case of first impression no costs should be awarded.  The respondent claimed and recovered costs in the amount of $10,000.  We are of the opinion that the appellant is entitled to its costs at first instance.  Consequently, the order awarding $10,000 costs to the respondent is set aside and in its place an order will go awarding the appellant costs of $10,000 all inclusive. 

[3]               The appellant is also entitled to the costs of this appeal which we hereby fix in the amount of $15,000 all inclusive.
