APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appellant is not here.  He seeks an adjournment.  Given the history of the matter, including this appellant’s failed attempt to secure an adjournment last week, the respondents oppose the adjournment request.  There is merit to this position, however, we do not have to decide whether the request for an adjournment is bona fide.  The appeal (and the later appeal C44999) are clearly moot.  The appellant is now subject to a further detention order issued by the Board on March 9, 2007.  All counsel agree that the appeals are moot.  The appeals are dismissed.
