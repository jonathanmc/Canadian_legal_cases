APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               In our view, the verdict was reasonable. The trial judge carefully set out the facts that formed the basis for finding that the appellant aided or abetted. We are satisfied that it was open to the trial judge to infer that the appellant aided or abetted the principal offender with the necessary mens rea based on those findings of fact.

[2]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.
