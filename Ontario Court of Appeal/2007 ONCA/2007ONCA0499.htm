COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]               On May 1, 2007, the court released its reasons for judgment in this matter, allowing the appeal and directing a new trial in accordance with our reasons.  We awarded the appellant, Consulate Ventures Inc., its costs of the appeal, as fixed by this court, and invited written submissions from the parties on the costs of the original trial.  We have now received and reviewed the written costs submissions of the appellant and correspondence from the respondents relating to the costs of the first trial.

[2]               In accordance with the agreement reached by the parties, the costs of the original trial before Macdonald J. of the Superior Court of Justice are reserved to the judge conducting the new trial that we have ordered.
