APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appeal is quashed.  The Children’s Lawyer does not have standing to bring this appeal.  While the Children’s Lawyer appeared on the motion below, she did so solely for the purpose of requesting that the motion be adjourned so that the Litigation Guardian could attend.  The Children’s Lawyer did not seek to be appointed Litigation Guardian, nor did she seek to intervene as a party.

[2]               The Litigation Guardian has not appealed the orders made below.  Hence, there is no proper appeal before this court.

[3]               Costs are awarded to each of the respondents fixed in the amount of $2,000 each, inclusive of disbursements and GST.
