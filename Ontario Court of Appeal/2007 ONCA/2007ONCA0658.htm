APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appellant submits that the trial judge misapprehended the evidence and failed to appreciate that the expert’s opinion was based on assumptions that were not borne out by the evidence. We do not agree. These arguments depend almost entirely on the appellant’s testimony being accepted. The trial judge rejected her evidence and with good reason; her story was highly improbable and fraught with inconsistencies.

[2]               The trial judge understood that none of the various factors on their own could prove that the admitted possession was, for the purpose of trafficking.

[3]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.
