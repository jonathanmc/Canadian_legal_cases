ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The motion judge followed Misko v. Doe, [2006] O.J. No. 3624, and denied the appellants leave to issue a third party claim.  The appellants conceded in oral argument that their appeal in this case rested on the proposition that Misko v. Doe was wrongly decided.  An appeal from that decision was dismissed by this court on September 25, 2007: [2007] ONCA 660.  It follows that this appeal must also be dismissed.  The respondents are entitled to costs fixed at $3,500 inclusive of disbursements and GST.
