APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The trial judge accepted the evidence of the respondent's principal concerning his company's costs and work orders and profit margins. In our view, there was sufficient documentary evidence filed at trial to support the respondent's claim and the trial judge's finding. The appeal is therefore dismissed.

[2]               Costs of the appeal are to the respondent in the amount $3494.34 on a partial indemnity scale inclusive of G.S.T. and disbursements.
