APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The reasons for sentence are not helpful and do not address some of the factors (e.g. the guilty plea and the rehabilitative efforts of the appellant) that should have been addressed.  In the end, however, we think that the total sentences imposed were fit in all of the circumstances.

[2]               We were pleased to hear that the appellant’s rehabilitation has moved forward and that he will be paroled in December.

[3]               Leave to appeal granted and the appeal is dismissed.
