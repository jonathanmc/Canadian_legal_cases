APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We agree with the Crown that the trial judge failed to apply the test in s. 490.012(4) of the Criminal Code.  We are not persuaded that there is any basis to refuse the order.

[2]               Accordingly, the appeal is allowed and an order shall issue under s. 490.012(1) of the Criminal Code requiring the respondent to register under Sex Offender Information Registration Act.
