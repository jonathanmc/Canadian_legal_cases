COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]               Following the release of the decision in this family law matter on September 27, 2007, we received submissions concerning the quantum of costs.  We have reviewed those submissions. 

[2]               Mrs. Higgins shall have her costs of the appeals brought against her by Mr. Higgins on a partial indemnity scale fixed in the amount of $3,000 inclusive of G.S.T. and disbursements payable by Mr. Higgins.

[3]               Ms. Fisher shall have her costs of the appeal brought by her against Mrs. Higgins and the costs of the appearance before Perkins J. on a partial indemnity scale fixed in the total amount of $1,500 inclusive of G.S.T. and disbursements payable by Mrs. Higgins.
