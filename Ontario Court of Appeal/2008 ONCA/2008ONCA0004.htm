APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appellant appeals from the judgment of Belobaba J. dated 18 July 2007 dismissing two actions brought by the appellant against the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Ontario and various doctors performing review functions under the Regulated Health Professions Act.

[2]               We see no merit in the appeal.  The motion judge’s analysis of the confidentiality and immunity from suit provisions in the RHPA and his application of the doctrine of res judicata strike us as sound.  His conclusion that both actions should be dismissed was correct.

[3]               The appeal is dismissed.  Costs to the respondents fixed at $5000 inclusive of disbursements and GST.
