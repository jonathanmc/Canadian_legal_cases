On appeal from the judgment of Justice George T. Valin of the Superior Court of Justice dated June 19, 2007.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appellant appeals the order of Valin J. dated 19 June 2007 striking out the portions of the statement of claim against the defendants George Windsor and Francis and Associates.

[2]               We entirely agree with Valin J.’s conclusion that “there are insufficient facts pleaded by the plaintiff in its amended statement of claim to support the bald allegation that Windsor made a negligent misrepresentation to it.”

[3]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.  The respondents are entitled to their costs fixed at $5000 inclusive of disbursements and GST.
