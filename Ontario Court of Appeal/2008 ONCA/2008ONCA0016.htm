APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appellant submits that the sentencing judge misapprehended evidence on sentencing. Specifically the appellant continued his abuse in relation to one, not both, victims following his release from jail for a similar offence. In addition, the appellant submits the sentencing judge failed to appreciate and to give proper weight to Dr. Langevin’s report and that his reasons reflect this. Even assuming that the sentencing judge erred in the manner submitted by the appellant, we specifically reject the appellant’s submission that he misapplied the decisions in R. v. D. (D.), [2002] O.J. No. 1061 ( C.A. ) and R. v. C. (T.), [2005] O.J. No. 24 (C.A.), the overall sentence was fit.
