APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               It is now 11:35 a.m.  Counsel for the appellant has been removed from the record on December 12, 2007.  The appellant is not here. Counsel for the respondent has advised that the appellant left for India in October and has apparently not returned.  There is an outstanding warrant for the arrest of the appellant.  In the circumstances, we think it is appropriate to dismiss the appeal as abandoned.  So ordered.  The respondent is entitled to her costs on a substantial indemnity basis for these proceedings.  Costs fixed at $20,335.62, inclusive of GST and disbursements.
