ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The parties have settled this appeal.  A term of the settlement requires that we allow the appeal and set aside the judgment of the trial judge. 

[2]               We have not had the benefit of a full argument of the appeal.  However, we have read the appellant’s factum and his counsel has taken us to those paragraphs that would justify our allowing the appeal and ordering a new trial. 

[3]               Counsel for the respondents has conceded that there is a basis for us to do so.  Taking into account the public interest in settling litigation, we are satisfied that it is appropriate to approve the order sought by the parties.

[4]               Accordingly, the appeal is allowed, the judgment of the trial judge is set aside, and the action is dismissed in accordance with the minutes of settlement.
