APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appellant did not appear before Ground J.  The issue of occupation rent was not raised before him and in our view, it is not the proper subject matter of this appeal. In all other respects, we see no error in Ground J.’s analysis or conclusion relating to the sale of the property.

[2]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed. Costs to the Trustee from the estate fixed at $7,000 inclusive of G.S.T. and disbursements.
