COSTS ENDORSEMENT  

[1]               Further to the court’s reasons for judgment in this matter issued on December 21, 2007 and February 6, 2008, we have received the written submissions of the respondent on costs.  No costs submissions were received from the appellants.

[2]               Having regard to the dismissal of this appeal and the unchallenged costs submissions of the respondent, we are of the view that the respondent should be awarded its costs of the appeal on a partial indemnity basis, fixed in the amount of $2,630.78, inclusive of disbursements and G.S.T., and we so order.
