APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               This appeal is fact driven. The trial judge found as a fact that the appellant was a partner in the family business and that she knowingly mortgaged her home to provide funds for that business.

[2]               In our view, those findings are supported by the evidence and they are fatal to the appellant’s claim that the respondent was negligent in failing to ensure that she received independent legal advice.

[3]               In view of this conclusion, it is unnecessary to address the abuse of process issue.

[4]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.

[5]               Costs to the respondent in the amount as agreed of $34,000 inclusive of GST and disbursements.
