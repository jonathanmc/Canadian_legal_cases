ADDENDUM

[1]               This court’s endorsement in this case was released on December 27, 2007.  Having received and reviewed the submissions of counsel for the appellant regarding the proposed transfer of this appeal to the Divisional Court, and having been informed that counsel for the respondents do not object thereto, this court’s endorsement is hereby amended, as follows:

1.      The first sentence of para. one of this court’s endorsement is amended to read: “In our view, this court lacks juris-diction to entertain this appeal.”

2.      The first sentence of para. three of this court’s endorse-ment is amended to read: “Accordingly, for the reasons given, this court is without jurisdiction to entertain this appeal and it is hereby transferred to the Divisional Court for determination.”

[2]               In all other respects, this court’s endorsement dated December 27, 2007 remains in full force and effect.
