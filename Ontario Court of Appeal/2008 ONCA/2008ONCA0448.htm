APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The trial judge gave sufficient reasons for his decision to remove and replace one executor, Mr. Dorino Susin, replacing him with Mr. Rahman, and not to remove and replace Mr. Fermino Susin. We see nor error in his reasoning or his conclusion. The costs award to the trustee was also appropriate in the circumstances. The appeal is therefore dismissed.

[2]               Costs of $3,500 inclusive of disbursements and G.S.T. payable by the appellant.
