APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The only issue argued on appeal from conviction was the lack of proof of continuity. The evidence of  continuity was established by direct and circumstantial evidence. We agree entirely with the complete reasons of Young J.

[2]               There was a serious offence involving smuggling heroin into a correctional facility. The sentence imposed was fit.

[3]               The appeal from conviction is dismissed. Leave to appeal sentence is refused.
