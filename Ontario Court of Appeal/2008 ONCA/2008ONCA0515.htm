APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The “defence” the appellant now advances, i.e. that Mrs. Dhir was not a party to the loan was not pleaded in the statement of defence or advanced at trial.  It is too late to advance that position now.  In any event, there is some evidence from which her participation in the loan could be inferred. 

[2]               The appeal is dismissed.  Costs to the respondent in the amount of $3,500, inclusive of disbursements and GST.
