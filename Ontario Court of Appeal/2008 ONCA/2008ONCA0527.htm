APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               Rule 37.07(1) requires service of the notice of motion on any party who will be affected by the order sought.  The appellant, as the holder of a certificate of pending litigation on the property, was clearly affected by the order.

[2]               Rule 37.14 allows a party affected by an order obtained without notice to move to set it aside.  The motion judge should have set aside the judgment obtained without notice.

[3]               The appeal is allowed.  Costs fixed in the amount of $10,000 inclusive of disbursements and GST.
