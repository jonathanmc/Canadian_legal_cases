APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We agree substantially with the reasons of DiTomaso J.  Even if he misstated the fourth branch of the Palmer test at para. 38 of his reasons by using the word “would” instead of “could”, we agree with his conclusion that the fresh evidence was not admissible.

[2]               The appeal is dismissed.
