APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We see no error in the decision of Hoilett J.   To the extent that the plaintiff wished to advance causes of action independent of his action for wrongful dismissal, he has not pleaded the material facts founding these claims.

[2]               He has amended on two prior occasions and has not provided a proposed statement of claim here. 

[3]               In these circumstances we see no error in Hoilett J. not granting leave to appeal nor would we grant leave to amend.  The appeal is dismissed.

[4]              Costs to the respondents in the amount of $2,500.00.
