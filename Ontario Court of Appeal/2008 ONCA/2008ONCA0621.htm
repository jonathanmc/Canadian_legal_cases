ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We have received costs submissions from the parties.  The appellants are entitled to their costs of the appeal fixed at $50,000 inclusive of disbursements and G.S.T.

[2]               The respondent Frontenac Ventures Corporation should pay $40,000 and the respondent Her Majesty the Queen in right of Ontario should pay $10,000.  The latter respondent should pay costs because, although its position on the appeal ultimately was that the appeal should be allowed and the sentences reduced, its position throughout the original contempt and sentencing hearings was instrumental in leading to the appeal.
