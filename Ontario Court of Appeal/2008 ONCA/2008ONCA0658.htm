APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               With respect to the learned motion judge, we are of the view that the appellant’s claim in this action is a separate independent tort not arising in respect of the worker’s injury but instead, from the employer’s alleged negligent misrepresentation regarding the process to be followed in pursuing the claim.

[2]               Accordingly, the appeal is allowed and the order dismissing the action is set aside.

[3]               Costs of the motion and the appeal to the appellant fixed in the total amount of $7,500 plus G.S.T.
