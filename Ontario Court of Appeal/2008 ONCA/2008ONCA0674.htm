APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               There is no merit to this appeal.  As Murray J. correctly concluded, the material filed failed to disclose a change in circumstances and failed to comply with rule 15(7) of the Family Law Rules regarding financial statements.

[2]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.

[3]               Costs of the appeal payable by the appellant to the respondent fixed in the amount of $500, inclusive of disbursements and G.S.T.
