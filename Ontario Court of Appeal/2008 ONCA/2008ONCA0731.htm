APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We do not admit the fresh evidence – it relates to issues not in play on this appeal.

[2]               We see no basis for interfering with Divisional Court’s order modifying the motion judge’s order imposing a condition relating to transportation and accommodation costs of the appellant when she travels to Windsor for these proceedings.  The modification simply puts in place a mechanism for judicial review of expenses incurred by the appellant, rather than leave those expenses open-ended and unreviewable.

[3]               The appeal is dismissed.

[4]               No costs.
