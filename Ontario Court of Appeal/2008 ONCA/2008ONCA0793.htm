ENDORSEMENT RE COSTS

[1]               We agree with Mr. Adair’s characterization of the issue raised on appeal as a dispute over the allocation of defence costs as between insurer and insured, and not a dispute over the insurer’s duty to defend.  The University of Western Ontario is entitled to costs on a partial indemnity basis.  We fix those costs at $25,000, inclusive of disbursements and GST.
