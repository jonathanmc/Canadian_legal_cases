APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               In our view there is no basis to interfere with the sentence. It followed a joint submission. The trial judge was aware of the appellant’s medical condition and the fresh evidence does not suggest that the correctional institute is unable to deal with her chronic conditions. The appeal is dismissed.
