APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appeal is dismissed for the reasons expressed by Festeryga J.  In our view if the Legislature had intended that the statutory regime established to resolve the distribution of statutory accident benefit losses as between insurers was to deprive injured parties of the rights to sue in tort actions, it would have said so.  It did not.
