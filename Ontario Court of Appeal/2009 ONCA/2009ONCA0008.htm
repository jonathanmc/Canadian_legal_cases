APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We agree with the careful reasons and conclusions of the motion judge. 

[2]               We are also satisfied that s. 46.1 of the Human Rights Code creates a new substantive jurisdiction and that it should be read prospectively only.  The section came into force in June 2008 and cannot reach the events in issue in this action. 

[3]               The appeal is dismissed.  Costs to the respondents in the amount of $1,500, inclusive of disbursements and GST.
