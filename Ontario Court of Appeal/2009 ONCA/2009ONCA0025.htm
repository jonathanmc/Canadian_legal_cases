An application for leave to appeal the decision of Justice John Jennings of the Superior Court of Justice, sitting as a summary conviction appeal judge, dated October 26, 2005, dismissing the conviction by Justice S. Gail Dobney of the Ontario Court of Justice dated May 12, 2003.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We have not been persuaded that this application for leave to appeal involves a question of law alone. The appeal judge made a  finding of fact that even if the trial lawyer had provided effective assistance, the result would have been the same. In our view, this was a finding of fact or mixed fact and law and was a finding that was reasonably open on this record. Accordingly, leave to appeal is refused.
