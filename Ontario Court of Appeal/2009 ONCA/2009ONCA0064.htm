APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We see no error on the part of Allen J.  On a previous motion, Spies J. carefully considered the appellants’ statement of claim and struck substantially all of the claims but gave leave to amend.  Spies J. also provided guidance as to the defects in the claim and how it might be amended.  Allen J. concluded that the present claim repeated allegation that had been struck by Spies J. , failed to remedy the defects identified by Spies J. and added additional claims none of which was viable.  We agree and see no basis to interfere.  Appeal dismissed.  Costs to the respondent fixed at $10,000 inclusive of disbursements and GST.
