APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The motion judge gave careful and thorough reasons.  We see no error in his decision on either of the points argued on the appeal. 

[2]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.  The respondents are entitled to their costs, which we fix as follows (inclusive of disbursements and GST):

·                    City of Mississauga - $2,500;

·                    Dixie Ford Sales Ltd. - $2,500;

·                    Ontario Realty Corporation - $10,000; and

·                    Tony Miele - $8,000.
