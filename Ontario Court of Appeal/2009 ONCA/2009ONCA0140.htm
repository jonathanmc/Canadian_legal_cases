ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The Crown appeals the 18 month sentences imposed on both respondents for money laundering offences.

[2]               Although we agree with the Crown that the sentencing judge erred by criticizing the police sting aspect of this case, contrary to the decision of the Supreme Court of Canada in R. v. Kirzner (1978), 38 C.C.C. (2d) 131 at p. 137, we do not think that error detracts from his careful review of the aggravating and mitigating factors.  We think that the trial judge’s review of these factors does not make the sentence he imposed manifestly unfit.

[3]               The appeal is dismissed.
