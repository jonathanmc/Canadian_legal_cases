[1]                Mr Xynnis rose at the opening of court today to ask for an adjournment to try to obtain fresh expert evidence. Mr. Xynnis took over the file recently from Mr. Koziebrocki who passed away in December.

As Mr. Xynnis agreed, the appeal will be adjourned to a peremptory date of April 9, 2009 with the same time allotment of one hour for the appellant and 40 minutes for the respondent.
