APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We see no error in the Minister’s surrender order.  Even accepting the appellant’s affidavit as fresh evidence, we are not persuaded that his evidence affords a basis to remit the issue of his surrender to the Minister.

[2]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.

[3]               The appeal from the committal order is dismissed as abandoned.
