APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We commend Ms. Samuels for the sincere efforts that she now appears to have taken to rehabilitate herself, especially to address her drug addition.  However, in our view, the sentence imposed by the trial judge was fit.

[2]               Accordingly, though leave to appeal sentence is granted, the sentence appeal is dismissed.

[3]               The appellant did not pursue her conviction appeal, and it is therefore dismissed.
