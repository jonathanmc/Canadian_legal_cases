APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appeal is dismissed.  The respondent concedes that there are no issues of res judicata or issue estoppel arising from the decision or reasons of the court below.  We agree.

[2]               The appellant is pursuing similar claims to those in this application in a separate action.  There was no basis to continue both the application and the action.  The judge below correctly dismissed the application.  Costs to the respondent are fixed in the amount of $5,000, inclusive of disbursements and GST.
