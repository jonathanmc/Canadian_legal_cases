APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               In our view, it was open to the trial judge on the record to conclude that the defence witness had failed to offer an explanation for what the trial judge determined was an important inconsistency in the evidence.  It was for the trial judge to decide what impact that failure should have on the witness’s credibility and we see no ground for interference by this court.

[2]               The appeal is dismissed.
