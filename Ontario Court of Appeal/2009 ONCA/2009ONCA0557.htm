APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               The appeal is allowed.  The Crown concedes that there was an error in the warrant for committal.  This error overstated the appellant’s sentence by 5 months.  As a result, the appellant was not permitted to seek early parole as the trial judge intended and as he would have been had the error not been made.

[2]               In the result, we accept the joint submission of counsel to reduce the custodial position of the sentence to time served.  In all other regards, the sentence remains the same.
