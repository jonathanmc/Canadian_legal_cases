APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The Public Guardian and Trustee appeals para. 5 of the order appointing it limited guardian of property for Carol Connor, and the corollary orders of Corbett J.  We agree with Ms. Fullerton that the appeal must be allowed because the preconditions in the Substitute Decisions Act for making the appointment have not been met.

[2]              Accordingly, the appeal is allowed and paras. 5, 6, 9 and 10 of the order of Corbett J. are set aside.  In their place there shall be an order that the money referred to in para. 4(C) be paid into court to the credit of Carol Connor, to be paid out on further order of the court.  Ms. Fullerton has advised the court that the Public Guardian and Trustee will assist Ms. Connor in dealing with the money.
