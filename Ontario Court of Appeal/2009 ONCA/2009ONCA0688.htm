APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The parties agree that the charges giving rise to the recognizance were withdrawn.  Accordingly, the appellant was not in fact bound by a recognizance on December 13, 2008, and was therefore not in breach of any court order by consuming alcohol.

[2]              Accordingly, the appeal on the count of breach of recognizance is allowed.
