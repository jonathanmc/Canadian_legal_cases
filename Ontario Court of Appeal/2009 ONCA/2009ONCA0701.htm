APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We see no error in the decision of the motion judge granting summary judgment. The two issues the appellant identifies as genuine issues for trial are matters on which the appellant already put his best foot forward in his application and on his cross examination. The evidence before the motion judge was sufficient to come to the legal conclusion he did.

[2]              The appeal is dismissed with costs of $3,000 inclusive of disbursements and G.S.T.
