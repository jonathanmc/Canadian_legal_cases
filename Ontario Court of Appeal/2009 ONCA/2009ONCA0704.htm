ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The appellant seeks to set aside the motion judge’s order striking his counterclaim in action number CV-08-7591-00CL (the “2008 action”).

[2]              It is uncontested that the claims advanced by the respondents against the appellant in the 2006 action, which action was eventually settled, overlap to some extent with the claims now sought to be pursued by the same respondents against the appellant in the 2008 action authorized under s. 38 of the Bankruptcy and Insolvency Act.  In these circumstances, we do not agree that the appellant’s proposed counterclaim simply attempts to relitigate the issues in the 2006 action.  On the contrary, by the matters raised in his proposed counterclaim, the appellant is seeking to prevent relitigation of the claims in the 2006 settled action.

[3]              Accordingly, the appeal is allowed and paragraph three of the motion judge’s order dated March 12, 2009 is set aside.  In all the circumstances, we award no costs in respect of this appeal.
