APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We agree with the decision of Morawetz J.  Investors claim is a personal tort action for damages against the appellants.  It does not assert a claim against an asset of Kaiser.  As the Trustee’s report makes clear, the Trustee would have an interest only if the court identifies a specific asset or assets belonging to Kaiser.

[2]              Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed, with costs fixed at $4,265.15, inclusive of disbursements and GST.
