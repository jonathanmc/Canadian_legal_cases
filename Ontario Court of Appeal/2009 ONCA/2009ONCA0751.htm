APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We agree with the motion judge that on the facts as pleaded, there is no foundation for a claim of civil conspiracy against the respondent solicitor.

[2]              We do not read the motion judge’s reasons as dismissing the claims against the other defendants.

[3]              The appeal is dismissed.  Costs to the respondent are fixed in the amount of $5,000, inclusive of GST and disbursements.
