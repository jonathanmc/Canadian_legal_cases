ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We are of the view that the motion judge did not err in refusing to set aside the order of Justice Boyko.  There had been a three-month delay in bringing the motion and no explanation for that delay.

[2]              We are also of the view that the motion judge did not err in ruling that rule 11.02 does not apply to the circumstances of this case.

[3]              Finally, given the defendants’ extensive delays in this action, we see no basis for interfering with the motion judge’s decision to strike the defendants’ pleadings for non-compliance with his earlier order of December 11, 2008.

[4]              The appeal is, therefore, dismissed.  Costs to the respondent fixed in the amount of $6,000, inclusive of disbursements and GST.
