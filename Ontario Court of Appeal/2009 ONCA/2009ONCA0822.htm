APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We would dismiss the appeal.  We see no error in the decision of Stewart J.  In respect of the arguments raised by the appellant, we state as follows:

(1) The statute prevails over the Rules of Court.  The rules in the statute were followed – notice was not required.

(2) Consequently, we see no breach of natural justice or procedural fairness.

(3) The judges below had jurisdiction to make the orders that they did.

[2]              Costs to the respondent fixed at $2500, all inclusive.
