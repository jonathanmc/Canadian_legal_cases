APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The motion judge was correct in holding that the applicable limitation period had expired.  The material filed by the appellant on the motion did not rise to the level of creating a genuine issue for trial concerning whether the appellant lacked the capacity to initiate proceedings within the limitation period.  The appeal is therefore dismissed.
