APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The Crown fairly concedes that the length of the conditional sentence (2 years less one day) was manifestly excessive in the circumstances and asks that we reduce it to twelve months. We accept that submission and would reduce the sentence accordingly. That will mean that when the appellant is released in respect of the offences in the companion appeal (C51056), he will have four months left on his conditional sentence. In all other respects, we would not interfere with the sentence imposed by the trial judge.
