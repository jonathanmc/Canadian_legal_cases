[1]              In the event that it was necessary to consider the Charter issues in this in absentia trial, we are of the view that at a minimum, both the trial judge and the judge on appeal erred in failing to consider s. 24(2) of the Charter.

[2]              Accordingly, we would allow the appeal, set aside the judgments below and order a new trial.
