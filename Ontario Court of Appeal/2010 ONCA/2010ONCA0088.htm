APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We agree with the motion judge that, given all the evidence before him, there is no genuine issue warranting a trial of the factual question of whether there was an offer of settlement made in July 8, 2008 that was not subject to any condition about the logistics of payment.  That offer was clearly made and accepted the next day.

[2]              The appeal is dismissed.  Costs to the respondent fixed at $3500 all inclusive.
