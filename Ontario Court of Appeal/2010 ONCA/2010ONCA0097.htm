APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The appellant relies on a collateral agreement in defence of the plaintiff’s claim on a promissory note, share purchase agreement and guarantee.  There is no evidence in the record of any collateral agreement.  In these circumstances, there is no basis for us to look behind the terms of the written document upon which the plaintiff sues.  In our view, the motion judge did not err in granting summary judgment.  Appeal dismissed.  Costs to the respondent fixed at $5,887.35 inclusive of disbursements and GST.
