APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We would not admit the fresh evidence:

1)     It was available at trial;

2)     It is entirely inconsistent with the defence actually advanced by the appellant at trial; and

3)     The fresh evidence consists of the wife’s affidavit.  Even as of today, the appellant has not claimed that he was provoked.

[2]              The proffered evidence does not merit the criteria for admissibility on appeal. 

[3]              The sentence imposed at trial was fit.  Leave to appeal granted and the appeal dismissed.
