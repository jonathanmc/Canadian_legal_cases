APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              Leave to appeal is denied. The appellant accepts that the proposed appeal does not raise a question of law that has significance to the administration of justice beyond the four corners of this case. As for the second test, the appellant has failed to satisfy us that the trial judge committed any error, let alone a “clear” error of law in his analysis or conclusion.

[2]              Accordingly, leave to appeal is denied.
