APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The appellant appeals from the judgment of Belobaba J. dated September 25, 2009.

[2]              We agree with Belobaba J. that the cause of action relating to breach of a duty of good faith, as pleaded, did not disclose a cause of action.  We note that Belobaba J. gave the appellant leave to amend its pleadings.

[3]              The appeal is dismissed.  Costs to the respondent fixed at $7,500 inclusive of disbursements and GST.
