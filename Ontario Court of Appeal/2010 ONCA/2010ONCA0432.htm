APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We appreciate the assistance of Mr. Snell who was asked by the court to assist the appellant on this appeal.  We see no merit to the appeal.  To the extent that Dangerous Offender designation carries a unique stigma (some might prefer to call it a message or label), it was entirely warranted in this case.

[2]               The appeal is dismissed.
