On appeal from the disposition entered by the Ontario Review Board dated November 26, 2009.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We see no error in the Board’s disposition.  We endorse the comments of the Hon. J.W. Brooke at para. 16 of his reasons.  We do not agree with amicus that the terms of para. 16 should have been incorporated as part of the formal order.

[2]              That said, all counsel acknowledge the need to take all reasonable steps to obtain the necessary linguistic assistance to enable the appellant to meaningfully participate if he so wishes in the rehabilitative efforts.

[3]              We join with counsel in urging the hospital to make best efforts to obtain the necessary help.  No doubt, the Board will monitor the progress.

[4]              The appeal is dismissed.
