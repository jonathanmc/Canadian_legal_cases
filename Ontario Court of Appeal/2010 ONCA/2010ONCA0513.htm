COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]               By our reasons for judgment in this matter issued on May 28, 2010, the parties were directed to submit written costs submissions to the Registrar of this court.  We have now been informed by counsel that the respondent consents to an award of the costs of the proceedings before this court in favour of the appellant, The Bradford West Gwillimbury/Innisfil Police Services Board (the “Board), in the total amount of $10,000, inclusive of disbursements and G.S.T.  We have further been informed that the Crown appellants do not seek costs in respect of these proceedings.

[2]               In our view, the agreed amount of costs is appropriate in this case.  Accordingly, the Board is entitled to costs from the respondent in the agreed total amount of $10,000, inclusive of disbursements and GST, and we so order.
