On two motions to quash the appeal from the order of Justice Deena F. Baltman of the Divisional Court dated March 15, 2010.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The moving parties have made motions to quash the appellant’s appeal pursuant to s. 134(3) of the Courts of Justice Act.

[2]              We agree with the moving parties that the appellant is in the wrong court. He has appealed an order of a single judge of the Divisional Court directly to this court. The appellant’s proper appeal route is to a panel of the Divisional Court on a motion under s. 21(5) of the Courts of Justice Act.

[3]              The appeal is quashed.

[4]              No costs.
