APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               We are not persuaded that this is a proper case to grant leave to appeal.

[2]               The legislation under consideration has been amended.  This case would not provide guidance for future cases under the new legislation.

[3]               Moreover, insofar as the previous legislation is concerned, we see nothing in the principles relied on by the Summary Conviction Appeal judge that requires further elaboration by this court.

[4]               Accordingly, leave to appeal is denied.
