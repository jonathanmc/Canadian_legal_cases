APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]               In our view, the appeal from conviction must be dismissed.

[2]               There was no air of realty to the defence of honest but mistaken belief and the trial judge was therefore not obliged to consider it.

[3]               The trial judge did not misapprehend the expert evidence. He used it, as he was entitled to, as some evidence capable of confirming the complainants evidence that there was anal intercourse and that she did not consent to having sexual relations with the appellant.

[4]               Finally, the appellant has not pointed to anything in the trial judges reasons or in the record that would show that the trial judge applied a higher level of scrutiny to the appellants evidence over that of the complainant.

[5]               Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.
