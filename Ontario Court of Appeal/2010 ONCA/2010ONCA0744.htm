APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We agree with the reasons of the application judge.  In addition, we agree with the respondent that this claim is barred by the exclusions and exceptions relating to leases. The flaw in the appellant’s argument is the failure to take into account the terms of the insurance policy which governs the right to indemnity.

[2]              Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed with costs fixed at $7,791.24 inclusive of disbursements and applicable taxes.
