APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The sentencing judge did not advert to pre-sentence custody in her reasons. As the reasons do not disclose whether she took pre-sentence custody into account, in our view, the reasons reflect an error in principle.

[2]              Leave to appeal is granted. The appeal is allowed and the sentence on the robbery charge is reduced by 80 days.
