APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              The trial judge should have expressly addressed the application of the Gladue principles:  see R. v. Kakekagamick.  The custodial portion of the sentence has been served and we would not interfere at this point in time.  The appellant seem to be doing well.  A parole plan has been proposed that includes a treatment program.  We strongly urge the parole authorities to implement that program as soon as possible.

[2]              The appeal is dismissed.
