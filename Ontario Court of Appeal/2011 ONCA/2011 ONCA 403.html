On appeal from the Disposition of the Ontario Review Board dated October 14, 2010, with reasons dated December 10, 2010.

APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              Without commenting on the final sentence of the Board’s reasons where the Board, in rejecting a conditional discharge, took into account the fact that the appellant “is presently treatment consent incapable”, the remainder of the Board’s decision discloses no error. Read as a whole, the Board’s decision in our view, is reasonable and amply supported by the evidence. We see no basis for interfering. Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed.
