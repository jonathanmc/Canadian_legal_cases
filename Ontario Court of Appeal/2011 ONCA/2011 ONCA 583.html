APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We agree with the parties that the reasons are insufficient and that the convictions cannot stand. However, that inadequacy of reasons does not affect a finding of simple possession on Count # 1.

[2]              Accordingly, the appeal on Counts 3, 4 and 5 is allowed. The convictions quashed and a new trial ordered.

[3]              On Count # 1, the appeal is dismissed and a conviction for simple possession substituted and the appellant sentenced to four months imprisonment (which we note has already been served).

[4]              As to the new trial, Crown counsel has stated that the Crown does not  intend to proceed with the new trial.

[5]              The orders under ss. 109 and 487.05.1 are vacated.
