APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              This is not a case where the appellant and the victim gave completely incompatible versions of what happened.  The trial judge’s reasons make clear that the photos of the victim supported the victim’s evidence and that the trial judge acted on that basis.

[2]              His reasons are sufficient for appellate review.  The appeal must be dismissed.
