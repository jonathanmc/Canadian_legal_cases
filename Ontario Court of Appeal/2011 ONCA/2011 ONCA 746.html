APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]              We will not allow Mr. Gavris to make representations for the appellants for the following reasons:

1)     a panel of this court has already held that he cannot do so (see endorsement of Blair J.A., supra), and we see no reason to revisit that decision; and 

2)     he was a witness in the proceeding.

[2]              In our view, the merits of this matter should be addressed without any further delay.  We have considered the arguments advanced on behalf of the appellants in the factum of their former counsel and we have reviewed the reasons of the application judge.  We see no merit to the appeal.

[3]              The appeal is dismissed.  Costs to the respondent in the amount of $8,000, inclusive of disbursements and HST.
