APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          We do not agree that the trial judge misapprehended or failed to appreciate material aspects of the evidence. The trial judge specifically addressed each of the point raised by the appellant on appeal, specifically, the location of the truck, the phone call from the apartment, the MSN message and the “squishy” penis and explained why she was persuaded that the evidence of the complainant was reliable and trustworthy.

[2]          While there were discrepancies in the evidence to be explained, at the end of the day, the evidence of the complaint was supported in significant respects.

[3]          The trial judge explained why she rejects KC’s evidence regarding the location of the truck. The mother’s evidence regarding the phone call and the MSN message significantly corroborated that of the complainant. Given the age and experience of the complainant at the time, her description of the penis does not undermine her story.

[4]          We see no error that would justify appellate intervention. Appeal dismissed.
