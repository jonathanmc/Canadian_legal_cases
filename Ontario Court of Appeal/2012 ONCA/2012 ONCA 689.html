COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]          Since releasing our reasons for judgment in this appeal we have received a written request from counsel for the respondent’s CB Richard Ellis Limited, John Lafontaine, Karen Pickernell, and Carl Lavoie that they be awarded costs of the appeal. Counsel for these parties filed a factum, but made no oral submissions on the appeal. In the circumstances, we agree with Mr. Pepall’s suggestion that a counsel fee in the same amount as that awarded to the other respondents would be duplicative. In our view these respondents should receive one half of the costs awarded to the other respondents, namely, $3750 inclusive of disbursements and taxes.
