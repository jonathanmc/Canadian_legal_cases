COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]          We have now received and considered the submissions of the parties as to costs. In our view, as the successful party, the respondent RSM Richter Inc. is entitled to costs. Those costs are payable by the appellant personally, not the estate of Robert Mander. In all the circumstances, we fix the costs of all proceedings including the motion, stay, appeal and cross-appeal at $50,000 inclusive of disbursements and taxes.
