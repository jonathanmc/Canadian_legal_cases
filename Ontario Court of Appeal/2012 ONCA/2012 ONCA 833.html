APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          We see no reversible error in the reason of Wilson J.  Further, we see no possibility that the appellant’s attack on the constitutionality of the legislation can succeed.

[2]          Finally, this is not a case in which leave to amend the claim should be granted.  It is plain and obvious that this claim, no matter how drafted, is doomed to fail.

[3]          The appeal is dismissed, with costs fixed at $3,000, inclusive of disbursements and applicable taxes.
