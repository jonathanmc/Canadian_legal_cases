COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]       The court has reviewed the costs submissions of the parties and orders that costs be as follows:

1)           No costs of the appeal against the third party.

2)           Costs of the certification motion remitted to the motion judge to be dealt with in light of this Court’s reasons.

3)           Costs of the appeal payable by the respondent to the appellant on a partial indemnity basis in the amount of $50,000 for fees, $10,000 for disbursements, and in addition the amount of the applicable taxes.
