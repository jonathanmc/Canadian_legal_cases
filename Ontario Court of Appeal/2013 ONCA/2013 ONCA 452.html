APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          In our view, it is clear that this order by its very terms does not finally resolve the issues between the parties, relating to the two children, which is the subject of this appeal.

[2]          It is therefor not a final order and jurisdiction lies with the Divisional Court.  Accordingly, the appeal is quashed. Children’s Lawyer does not seek costs.  Costs to the respondent C.N. fixed in the sum of $2,000.00 inclusive of disbursements and HST.
