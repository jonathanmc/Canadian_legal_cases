APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The order is interlocutory.  We have no jurisdiction to hear the appeal.  In so holding, we stress that the decision of the motion judge is limited to the finding that the pleadings survive the “plain and obvious” test.  Nothing he said is determinative of any of the issues that will have to be decided at trial:  see R.S. v. R.H. (2000), 52 O.R. (3d) 152 (C.A.). 

[2]          The appeal is quashed.  Counsel are agreed that costs should be fixed at $5,000, payable in the cause.
