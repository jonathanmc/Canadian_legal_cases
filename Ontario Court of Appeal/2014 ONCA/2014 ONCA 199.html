APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          We agree with the Attorney General that this appeal is moot and that it should be dismissed on that basis. The appellant has been moved to the level of security he seeks on habeas corpus and, to the extent that his concern relates to his corrections record, there are alternate and more appropriate remedies.
