APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The Crown very fairly concedes that if the court is prepared to accept the fresh evidence, this appeal should be allowed. 

[2]          Based on our review of the fresh evidence and the transcript of the guilty plea, we are satisfied that fresh evidence meets the Palmer criteria.

[3]          The appeal is therefore allowed, the conviction set aside and a new trial ordered.
