APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          Leave to appeal is denied.  No question of law of general importance has been raised.  Moreover, the appellant has failed to demonstrate clear legal error in the summary conviction appeal court judge’s reasons.  The summary conviction appeal court judge considered the arguments raised and found them to be without merit.  We agree.
