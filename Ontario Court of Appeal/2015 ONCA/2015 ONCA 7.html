By the Court:

[2]          By letter dated January 5, 2015, counsel for EllisDon questioned the intention of the court and asked whether EllisDon has two more years from the date of this court’s decision obtain a legislative solution.

[3]          The court’s intention was that the new two-year estoppel period originally set by the OLRB would start again on the date of this court’s decision.
