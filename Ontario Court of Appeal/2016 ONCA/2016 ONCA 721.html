COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]          We have reviewed the costs submissions.  The appellant was substantially, but not entirely successful on appeal.  The appellant should have its costs on a partial indemnity basis.  Having regard to the nature of the motion and the result, we fix those costs at $7,500, inclusive of disbursements and applicable taxes.

[2]          With respect to the costs of the motion, we agree with the respondent’s submission that it remains the successful party on the motion and is entitled to costs on the motion.  We decline to tinker with those costs to reflect the result of the appeal.  The fact remains that the respondent successfully disposed of a significant part of the claim by bringing the motion to strike.  The costs order made by the motion judge stands.
