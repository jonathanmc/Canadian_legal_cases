APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The Crown agrees that the appeal be allowed in respect of the error in credit accorded for pre-sentence custody. Leave to appeal sentence is granted, the appeal is allowed, pre-sentence custody is to be accorded credit of 1.5 to 1 resulting in a reduction of the sentence from 10 years 290 days to 9 years 251 days crediting pre-trial custody of 3 years, 114 days instead of 2 years 75 days.
