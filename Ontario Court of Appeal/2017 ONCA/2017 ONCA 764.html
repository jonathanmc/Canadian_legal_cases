APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The appellant has not appeared. The court attempted to reach him at the telephone numbers provided and were told that the first number was his brother’s number. The brother provided a second number to the court which number when tried was no longer in service. In addition, counsel for the respondent sent an email to the address provided and received no response. Having reviewed all of the materials in preparation for the appeal we are of the view that there is no merit in this appeal.

[2]          Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed. Costs to the respondent fixed in the sum of $7,533.60 inclusive of disbursements and HST.

[3]          An order to issue dispensing with the need for counsel for the respondent to obtain the appellant’s approval of the order relating to this endorsement.
