APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

The Crown concedes that the trial judge committed reversible error in his application of the rule in Browne v. Dunn, necessitating a new trial.

We agree that the trial judge committed reversible error. Accordingly, the appeal is allowed and a new trial is ordered on the sexual assault charge.

In the circumstances, the appellant’s application to introduce fresh evidence going to the issue of ineffective assistance of counsel is dismissed as moot.
