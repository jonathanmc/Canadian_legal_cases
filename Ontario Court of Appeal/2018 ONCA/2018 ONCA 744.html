APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The order appealed from is for an amount less than $50,000. Such an appeal lies to the Divisional Court pursuant to s. 19(1)(a) of the Courts of Justice Act. It is the amount awarded and not the amount that was claimed that determines jurisdiction: see McGrath v. Woodrow (2001), 52 OR (3d) 732 (C.A.) para. 16. As a result, the appeal is quashed. Costs to the respondent fixed in the amount of $2,500 inclusive of disbursements and applicable taxes.
