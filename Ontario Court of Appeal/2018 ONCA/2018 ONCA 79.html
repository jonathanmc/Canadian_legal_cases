[3]          We note that Legal Aid Ontario denied funding for a conviction appeal based on an ineffective assistance of counsel in 2014. It does not appear that the possibility of advancing that ground has been pursued since.

[4]          In our view, it is unadvisable to hear the sentence appeal before the conviction appeal.

[5]          The sentence appeal shall remain in the regular appeal stream, and subject to further court order, shall not be rescheduled for argument until the conviction appeal has been disposed of.
