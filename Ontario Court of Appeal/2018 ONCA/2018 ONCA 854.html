COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The respondents were successful on the appeal and are entitled to costs on the appeal.

[2]          While we do not consider the “presumption” in s. 137.1(7) of the Courts of Justice Act, R.S.O. 1990, c. C.43 as applicable on appeal, we do have regard to the nature of the suit in fixing costs of the appeal. In our view, this suit had the markings of a true SLAPP. We award costs of the appeal on a full indemnity basis and fix those costs at $40,000, inclusive of taxes and disbursements.
