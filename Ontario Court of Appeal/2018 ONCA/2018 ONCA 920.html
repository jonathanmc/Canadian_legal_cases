APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The appellant seeks to overturn the trial judge’s well-documented findings of fact and credibility. It is well-established that it is not the role of this court to retry cases. The appellant has failed to demonstrate any error of law or palpable and overriding error of fact in the trial judge’s assessment of the evidence and application of governing principles. The appeal is dismissed. Costs of $4,500 inclusive of disbursements and applicable taxes payable by the respondent to the appellant.
