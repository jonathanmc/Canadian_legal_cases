APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The appellant appeals from the endorsement of the motion judge dismissing his action pursuant to r. 2.1.01.

[2]          We see no basis to interfere with the decision of the motion judge. The essence of the appellant’s claim is that the Ottawa Police Service ought to have prevented an unnamed person from putting up posters stating that he was a spy. The motion judge correctly concluded that the respondent is not responsible at law for the torts of the officers of the Ottawa Police Service. The claim does not disclose a cause of action and is frivolous, vexatious and an abuse of process. The appeal is dismissed. The appellant shall pay the respondent’s costs of the appeal in the all-inclusive sum of $100.
