On appeal from the conviction entered on June 17, 2018 by Justice Robert Charney of the Superior Court of Justice.

REASONS FOR DECISION

[1]          We are of the view this appeal has no merit.

[2]          We agree with the motion judge’s analysis and conclusions that the police had reasonable and probable grounds to arrest the appellant; that his s. 10(b) right was not violated; and that the appellant’s statement en route to the cell was voluntary.

[3]          We need not deal with the other issues.

[4]          The appeal is dismissed.
