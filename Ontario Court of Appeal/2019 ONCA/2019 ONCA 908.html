APPEAL BOOK ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The appellants appeal from an order for sale under the Partition Act, R.S.O. 1990, c. P.4. Under s. 7 of the Partition Act this appeal lies to the Divisional Court. The appeal is therefore quashed for want of jurisdiction.

[2]          Costs of the appeal are to the respondent on a partial indemnity scale fixed in the amount of $10,000 inclusive of disbursements and taxes.
