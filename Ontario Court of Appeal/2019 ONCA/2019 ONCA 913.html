REASONS FOR DECISION

[1]           The appellants appeal from an order granting the respondent’s motion to amend her statement of claim in this Simplified Rules action. The order appealed from is interlocutory in nature and therefore this court does not have jurisdiction. Jurisdiction lies to the Divisional Court with leave: see Prescott & Russell (United Counties) v. David S. Laflamme Construction Inc., [2018] O.J. No. 2873.

[2]           The appeal is quashed for want of jurisdiction. The appellants are to pay the respondent’s costs fixed in the amount of $3200, inclusive of disbursements and applicable tax.
