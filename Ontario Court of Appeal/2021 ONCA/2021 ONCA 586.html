COSTS ENDORSEMENT

[1]          The court has received and reviewed the costs submissions of the parties in respect of the appeal, which was dismissed on July 22, 2021.

[2]          The respondent’s reliance on the circumstances of the intervention motion before Fairburn A.C.J.O., in respect of which no costs were awarded, is misplaced. Nor are there any other circumstances in relation to the appeal that would warrant an award of substantial indemnity costs.

[3]          Costs of the appeal, including the motion for fresh evidence, are fixed in favour of the respondent in the sum of $22,500, inclusive of disbursements and HST.
